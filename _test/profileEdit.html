    ICEMAN




    OVERVIEW — What to do...

    Date Assigned: December 31st, 1969

    As the current handler of Iceman, you will be fleshing out him character profile from the ground up. You are responsible for crafting his every character detail, from his name, to his skills and abilities, to his character history. We encourage you to think about him in all of his potential character dimensions; How did he get his powers? Does his keep it a secret? Has his shared his secret with anyone? Does his want to be a hero? Does his have any life goals beyond heroing? What about his family? We know this seems like a lot to do, and we recognize that it is a lot of potential work. Hence we've developed a lot of systems to help you along the way from drop-down selects, character asset libraries for you to draw on, to autoloading descriptions, etc. Heck, if you select a playby from our image library, you'll find that our system will autoload those images from our library into your profile when in view mode. However the edit mode will not show this so that it is clear that no images have actually yet been assigned. We believe that it is actually possible to complete a first draft of a character profile in less then an hour - good luck and we hope you have fun working on the Iceman's profile!

    History Click to see helpful hints on how to fill this section out!

    As a general rule, when developing your background, try to be dramatic but not over the top. In the long run, it is more interesting to have weaknesses, barriers to overcome, and difficult goals to fulfill than to have abundance, power, or effortless strength (this last note is just as, if not more important for game masters). Ideally, you want to address four domains in your background:

    A) Conflict - Who or what does your charcter fight for or against?

    B) CHALLENGES - What limits your character, physically, emotionally, and psychologically? Enquiring minds want to know!

    C) MYSTERY - What odd circumstnaces and unanswered questions linger in the past of XXXcNameXXX?

    D) PASSION - What motivates and drives XXXcNameXXX?

    Minimum Required Word Count: 800+ words

    1. HISTORY: As noted we expect a reasonable character history which HAS BEEN spellchecked and is written in the third person. XXXcNameXXX\'s history should reflect a brief review of the characters life before and after their heroic circumstances or transition from average citizen to focal character. It should showcase the origin of the character and up to the moment the game begins.
